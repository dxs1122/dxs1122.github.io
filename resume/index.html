About

David Sneider, trumpet, is majoring in Studio Music and Jazz at the University of Miamiâ€™s Frost School of Music, where he is a a member of the award-winning Stamps Jazz Quintet under the direction of professor Chuck Bergeron. He is currently studying jazz trumpet privately with Brian Lynch. Born to a family of musicians, David grew up right next to the New York jazz scene. He has performed with such artists as Gary Smulyan, Dick Oatts, Sean Jones, Avishai Cohen, Dave Holland, Pete Malinverni, Joel Frahm, and Brian Lynch. In addition, he has received notable recognitions as 1st place winner of the 2019 National Trumpet Competition, Outstanding Soloist in the 2017 DownBeat Student Music Awards, honorable mention in 2018 from the National YoungArts Foundation, Best Soloist three years in a row at the Sleepy Hollow Jazz Festival, and Outstanding Soloist at the 2018 Next Generation Jazz Festival.
