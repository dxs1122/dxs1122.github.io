About

David Sneider, is a jazz trumpet player and composer who is majoring in Studio Music and Jazz at the University of Miamiâ€™s Frost School of Music. At this university, he is a a member of the award-winning Stamps Jazz Quintet under the direction of professor Chuck Bergeron and he is studying his instrument privately with Brian Lynch. The son of a jazz trumpet player and opera singer, Sneider has been immersed in the New York music scene his whole life. He has recently performed with such artists as Gary Smulyan, Dick Oatts, Sean Jones, Avishai Cohen, Dave Holland, Joel Frahm and Larry Goldings. In addition, he has received notable recognitions as the 1st place winner of the 2019 National Trumpet Competition, Outstanding Soloist in the 2017 DownBeat Student Music Awards, honorable mention in 2018 from the National YoungArts Foundation, Best Soloist three years in a row at the Sleepy Hollow Jazz Festival, and Outstanding Soloist at the 2018 Next Generation Jazz Festival.
